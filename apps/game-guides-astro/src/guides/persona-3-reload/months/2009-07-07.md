**Dark Hour**

It’s time for your 4th Major Arcana boss. I’d highly recommend leaving Mitsuru behind for this one, make sure everyone has Ascetic Charms (Resist Charm), and that Yukari is wearing the Muscle Shirt Armour. Hopefully you brought Dis-Charms from the pharmacy.

You will be running around for a little bit before you get to the boss, just follow the ! Point on the map.
