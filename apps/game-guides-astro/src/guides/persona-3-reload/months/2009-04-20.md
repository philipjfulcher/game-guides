
**Dark Hour**

Tartarus exploration and combat tutorial. The last shadow that you fight in the tutorial will give you a shuffle time where you can choose from multiple cards, grab the yen card.

(Fool Rank 2)

> **Important! Important!**
> There is a locked chest that contains 2x Snuff Souls, these restore SP. SP restorative items are worth their weight in gold at this stage of this game.
