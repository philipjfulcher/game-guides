\***\*\*\*\*\***\_\_\_\_\***\*\*\*\*\***| Walkthrough |\***\*\*\*\*\***\_\_\_\_\***\*\*\*\*\***
\\\***\*\*\*\*\*\*\***\_\***\*\*\*\*\*\*\***//

The default order to play in is Terra, Ventus, then Aqua and the guide will
cover their stories in that order. The guide is written for the Critical
difficulty mode so all strategies are based on that. If at any time you feel
that the enemy is too difficult for you take some time to level your character
and his/her abilities to make the fight easier.

After the game's opening scenes you take control of Ventus. Run to the next
area and attack the ring with X. Next you use a command with triangle and then
lock on to a ring with L and R. Jump to the treasure chest and open it with X.
Head up and out of the area for a scene. Next you take control of Terra, block
Ventus' attack with square. Attack Ventus to build your Command Gauge and use
a Finish. With Aqua use a Shotlock with L and R held down. After that choose a
character to fight with and defeat your opponent. Move to the save menu and save
if you want. Afterwards you are brought to the character selection screen and
the end of the tutorial.
