title: Ventus' Reports
order: 7

You can complete all but the Arena Missions 100% with Ventus without playing
the other characters. After you beat the game with Terra and Aqua you can
return to the Arena Missions and complete the three Arena Mode battles, "Wheels
of Misfortune", "Weaver Fever", and "A Time to Chill" to earn 100% for Ventus.

## STORY

To earn a 100% in this section you must have all of the stories completed for
each section. Below are the sections in the same order the game lists:

- The Land of Departure
- Dwarf Woodlands
- Castle of Dreams
- Enchanted Dominion
- The Badlands
- Radiant Garden
- Disney Town
- Olympus Coliseum
- Deep Space
- Never Land
- The Mysterious Tower
- The Badlands
- The Land of Departure
- Destiny Islands

## SECRET REPORTS

- Xehanort's Letter

## STICKER ALBUM

There are three sections to the Sticker Album. The one that matters is the
actual Sticker Collection, the others are completed once you place the stickers
on the picture. To obtain 100% simply collect all 20 stickers and place them to
receive the items. You'll need to score 7 points with each sticker, so place
them in meaningful spots.

20p - Wellspring Crystal
40p - Fireworks
70p - Collision Magnet
110p - Salvation
