title: TOWER OF THE MOON
order: 0
---



The Tower of the Moon has some of the toughest monsters you have seen yet.
Heyedras. Terrordactyls have a chance of attacking twice. Watch out for
Whackolytes that can cast Kasap, Kabuff and Sizzle.



**Floor 1F**


Head west to the Treasure Chest to receive a SEED OF STRENGTH. Ascend the nearby
stairs.



**Floor 2F**


Make your way to the Treasure Chest to your east. It contains a SEED OF MAGIC.
Ascend the western stairs.



**Floor 3F**


Open the Treasure Chest for ~1278G. Head back down the stairs.



**Floor 2F**


Go southeast and down the stairs you see here.



**Floor 1F**


Open the Treasure Chest for a SEED OF RESILIENCE. Go back upstairs.



**Floor 2F**


Head northwest and ascend the stairs in the middle here.



**Floor 3F**


Head north and go up the stairs.



**Floor 4F**


Head south to the Treasure Chests where you will get ~243G and another SEED OF
RESILIENCE. Head northeast to the stairs.



**Floor 5F**


Ascend the stairs directly to the southwest.



**Floor 6F**


Head to the next stairs. Be careful not to fall off the edge.



**Floor 7F**


Collect the PRAYER RING from the Treasure Chest, then backtrack to Floor 5F
where you saw the doors.



**Floor 5F**


Open one of the doors and head down four flights of stairs.



**Floor 1F**


Talk to the guy if you want, then open the Chest to receive the MOONSHARD, the
main item you are looking for in this Tower. Evac out or stay for a bit if you
want to grind some experience and gold.
