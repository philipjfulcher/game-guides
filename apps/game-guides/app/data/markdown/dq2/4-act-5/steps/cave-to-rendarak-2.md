---
title: CAVE TO RENDARAK 2
order: 6
---



After you have rested up, head back to the Cave to Rendarak via the Teleportal
in Beran.



**Floor 1F**


There is a trap floor 5 steps east and 5 steps north of the stairs to the east
of where you start. Avoid that spot and make your way northeast to the eastern
stairs.



**Floor 2F**


Walk east and take the third path going north to the stairs.



**Floor 3F**


Walk as far north as you can, then take the path going east to the stairs.



**Floor 4F**


Final Green Dragon warning. From here on out, you will be going through their
territory. Quick Save often and stay healthy. Do not worry about using MP
because you saved your Prayer Rings for this. Climb the stairs.



**Floor 5F**


From the stairs, head 2 steps north, 4 steps west, 2 steps south, 3 steps west,
3 steps south and 1 step east. Now walk south to the wall, east to the wall and
north 1 step to go up the stairs.



**Floor 6F**


On this floor you will be presented with many branches of paths. Taking the
wrong ones will bring you back to where you are now.

Start by heading east.

At the first fork, go west.

At the second fork, go east.

Cross this room via the southernmost path and take the eastern way out of the
room.

At this fork, take the northeast path.

Take the northwestern path.

Take the northern path.

Take the eastern path. Continue east until you reach the stairs.
