---
title: SHRINE OF RUBISS
order: 2
---



You should now have the False Idol and all five Sigils. Your next destination is
the Shrine of Rubiss, northwest of Dirkandor. Sail or Zoom to Dirkandor. From
the mouth of the river, sail directly north to the Shrine.

In here, go down six flights of stairs to the bottom floor. Walk 4 steps north
to trigger a message. Read the dialogue, then check your Items. The Eye of
Rubiss should be in your possession. Leave the Shrine.
