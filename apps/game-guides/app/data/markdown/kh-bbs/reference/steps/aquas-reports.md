---
title: Aqua's Reports
order: 8
---



You can complete all but the Arena Missions 100% with Terra without playing
the other characters. After you beat the game with Terra you can return to the
Arena Missions and complete the Arena Mode battle "Weaver Fever" to earn 100%
for Aqua.

## STORY ##

To earn a 100% in this section you must have all of the stories completed for
each section. Below are the sections in the same order the game lists:

- The Land of Departure
- Castle of Dreams
- Dwarf Woodlands
- Enchanted Dominion
- Radiant Garden
- Disney Town
- Olympus Coliseum
- Deep Space
- Never Land
- Destiny Islands
- The Mysterious Tower

## SECRET REPORTS ##

To earn a 100% in this section you must find all four possible documents in
Aqua's game.

- Xehanort's Report 3
- Xehanort's Report 4
- Xehanort's Report 6
- Xehanort's Report 7

## STICKER ALBUM ##

There are three sections to the Sticker Album. The one that matters is the
actual Sticker Collection, the others are completed once you place the stickers
on the picture. To obtain 100% simply collect all 20 stickers and place them to
receive the items. You'll need to score 7 points with each sticker, so place
them in meaningful spots.

## Points Redeemable ##

20p - Ignite
40p - Shimmering Crystal
70p - Stop Barrier
110p - Deep Freeze
140p - Rhythm Mixer
